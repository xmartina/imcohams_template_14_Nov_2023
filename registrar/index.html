Registrar

  1. Home
  2. Registrar

Barr. Gogo Chisom Philips read law at the prestigious faculty of Law University of Nigeria Enugu Campus from 2008 to 2013 where he bagged the LLb (Hons.) degree. He proceeded to the Nigerian Law School where he did his vocational training and was called to bar and was enrolled as Barrister and solicitor of the Supreme Court in 2014.

Barr. Gogo Chisom Philips is a legal Adviser to many Companies, Banks and Clubs, he worked briefly with Asiegbu chambers up to 2016 before resigning to set up his private Legal Practice in Enugu.

Barr. Gogo Chisom Philips has attended many Conferences, Seminars and workshops, some of it boarded on human rights and Health laws and Practice in particular and also on Commerce and Business Management. He is the Proprietor of Renaissance Academy Groups of Schools Ebenator Ekwe and Umundugba Campuses.

Barr. Gogo Chisom Philips is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association. He has written other book including a best interesting titled “Dead Penalty and Fundamental human right” an appraisal amongst others.

Barr. Gogo Chisom Philips was born on 3rd day of November 1987 into the Gogo’s family of Okporo Umundugba Isu Local Government Area in Imo State of Nigeria. Between 2013 till date he is elected as the youth President, Grace of God Mission International Incorporated Orlu Divisional Head quarters Umundugba, youth President Umundugba Autonomous Community 2017 till date, youth Chairman Okporo Okabi Village, 2008 till date, a member of students Representative Council (SRC) in Nigeria law School yola Campus 2013 – 2014.
