Admission Form into Imo State College of Health and Management Sciences Amaigbo is Ongoing for the following Programs;

  • Diploma Programme
  • Higher National Diploma Programme (HND)
  • Certificate Programme

ONLINE SALE OF ADMISSION FORM PROCESS:

1. Click on ‘Portal’

2. Click on “Admissions”.

3. Click on “Apply for 2023/2024 Admissions.

4. Click to Generate Payment Invoice.
5. On the Generate invoice payment page, Select your programme type. Fill in your details ACCURATELY and click on the “Submit button”
4. Choose your payment mode (Pay with your bank ATM Card)

  • Click on Click here to pay with your ATM card on the generated invoice
  • Under “How do you want to pay” Select the ATM card type you have for payment
  • Under “Card Number” Enter the 16 or 19 digits ATM card number in front of your card
  • Enter Card expiry date
  • Turn the back of the card and enter the 3-digit number as the CVV
  • Click on Submit
  • Enter the one-Time password sent to your phone through SMS

5. Then click on Submit to complete your Payment

6. The RRR number will be automatically generated, click on submit to complete the Online Application Form
9. Review the application details supplied before submitting the online application.
10. Print ‘Acknowledgement Slip’
11. Await Response from the College.

STEP 1: ONLINE REGISTRATION/BIO-DATA UPDATE FOR NEW STUDENT

  • Visit the school website on: www.imcohams-edu.ng
  • Click on the School Portal
  • Click on Verify Account under Account Login page
  • Enter your Application Number as your matric number and a functioning e-mail as recovery email
  • Click on verify. (An account notification page will populate containing your Login details).
  • On the Account Notification Page, Click on login to display the Login Page
  • On the Login Page, Enter your Application Number as your username and Default Password and
  • Click on Login. (Change your password page will display, prompting you to change your password before you can proceed)
  • Enter your Application Number as your Old password and enter a New Password of your choice and
  • Click on Change Password (It will pre-load and return you to the login page) then,
  • Enter your Application Number as your username and your new password
  • Click on Login to Logon to your profile.
  • Click on Profile
  • Select Fill Profile Form (A Student Information Page will display)
  • Supply all the required information and click on Submit to complete your Bio-date registration
  • Refresh your portal account page or Logout and Login to view the available payment module and other Registration Steps to Complete.
  • (Other Steps Process flow to guide you on how to complete the available portal payment and course registration shall be updated in the school website or notice board. For more information frequent the school website and read through news or contact our support line on 08163728036
